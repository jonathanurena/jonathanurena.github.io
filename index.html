The Idea

We built Fiinly for the person who's tired of being afraid to look at their bank account.

Your data stays with you. We never see it. We never sell it. We never profit from it.

Instead of cold, automated syncing that leaves you disconnected, Fiinly encourages a short mindful log of your purchases. To make this easy, our Onboard AI works in the background—it listens when you speak, type, or take a photo, categorizing your life instantly so you don't have to navigate a spreadsheet.

A beautiful dashboard shows you everything at once — where your money goes, what you owe, and how close you are to free. Smart reminders tell you when a bill is coming before it becomes a problem. No surprises. No late fees. No shame.

And for the first time, a real date. The exact day your debt is gone, if you follow your customized plan. Not a guess. Not a projection. A date you can circle. Count down to. Hold onto.

Fiinly puts control back in your hands.

Our Promise

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Most financial apps exist inside a larger machine. Your spending habits have value to advertisers, to partners, to people you'll never meet. That's not a bug. It's the model. And it works against you.

Fiinly is different. We earn when you pay for the app. Nothing more. Your data never leaves your device—even our AI processes your entries locally. We don't know what you spend. We don't want to. The moment we profit from your habits, we stop being on your side.
