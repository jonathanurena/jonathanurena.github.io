This Privacy Policy describes how STRDE ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to handling your data with full transparency and in compliance with applicable privacy laws — including the GDPR, CCPA, and other regional regulations. By using STRDE, you agree to the practices described here.

01

Overview

STRDE is a productivity application designed to help users manage their schedules, activities, and goals. We operate with a privacy-first philosophy: wherever possible, your data stays on your device. We only process or transmit data when strictly necessary to provide core features — such as Google service integration — and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

This Policy applies to all users of the STRDE mobile application across all supported platforms and versions.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our services.

Information You Provide

  • Account credentials and profile information (name, email address) obtained through Google Sign-In
  • Content you create within the App, including schedules, entries, and notes
  • Preferences and settings you configure within the App

Information Collected Automatically

Device Info
Device model, OS version, and identifiers used for diagnostic purposes.
App Diagnostics
Crash logs and error reports to identify and resolve technical issues.
Usage Patterns
Aggregated, anonymized feature usage data to improve the App over time.
Network Info
General connectivity status to sync with integrated Google services.

Important: All content you create — entries, schedules, notes — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use a feature requiring external sync.

03

Google API & Services

STRDE integrates with Google APIs to provide authentication and calendar functionality. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Powered by Google API Services

Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive your basic profile information — typically your name, email address, and profile picture — solely for the purpose of creating and managing your STRDE account. This information is not used for advertising or shared with unauthorized third parties.

Google Calendar

STRDE may request access to your Google Calendar to enable scheduling features. Specifically:

  • We request only the minimum permissions necessary to read and write calendar events for STRDE's core functionality
  • We do not access or store calendar data beyond what is required for the features you actively use
  • Calendar data obtained through Google APIs is not shared with third parties
  • You may revoke STRDE's access at any time via Google Account Permissions

Scope of Google API Access

STRDE does not use Google user data to develop, improve, or train AI or machine learning models. Data obtained through Google APIs is used exclusively to operate the features you have enabled and is not transferred to third parties except as necessary to provide those features.

04

On-Device Data Storage

STRDE is built with a local-first architecture. The vast majority of data generated by your use of the App resides exclusively on your device:

  • Personal entries, goals, and activity logs are stored locally in the App's sandboxed storage environment
  • Local data is not transmitted to STRDE's servers unless you initiate a specific sync or export action
  • Your data is protected by your device's native security mechanisms, including OS-level encryption
  • Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored App data from your device

We strongly recommend keeping your device OS up to date and enabling device-level encryption — such as a passcode, Face ID, or fingerprint lock — to protect locally stored data from unauthorized access.

05

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the App — Core functionality, authentication, and feature delivery
  • Personalize your experience — Applying your preferences and settings across sessions
  • Improve the App — Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to identify improvements
  • Ensure technical stability — Diagnosing crashes, errors, and performance issues
  • Communicate with you — Sending important updates about the App or this Policy, where you have opted in
  • Comply with legal obligations — Meeting applicable legal requirements and enforcing our Terms of Service

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, profiling, or sale to data brokers.

06

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers — Trusted vendors who assist in operating the App, under strict contractual data protection obligations
  • Google LLC — As necessary to facilitate Google Sign-In and Calendar integration, governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Legal Requirements — When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental authority
  • Protection of Rights — To protect the rights, property, or safety of STRDE, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfers — In connection with a merger or acquisition, provided the acquiring entity honors this Policy
07

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data we handle:

  • Encrypted data transmission using industry-standard TLS protocols for any data exchanged with external services
  • Secure token-based authentication for Google API access, stored in platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Access controls limiting employee access to user data to only those with a legitimate operational need
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our practices as the threat landscape evolves

While we take data security seriously, no system is completely impenetrable. We encourage you to use a strong device passcode and to report any suspected security issues to us promptly.

08

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by applicable law:

  • On-device data — Retained until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App
  • Account data (Google Sign-In) — Retained for the duration of your active account; deleted upon request
  • Crash and diagnostic logs — Retained for up to 90 days for debugging, then automatically purged
  • Anonymized analytics — May be retained indefinitely as they cannot be used to identify you

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us using the information in Section 12.

09

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection / Restriction — Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent — Withdraw consent for data processing activities at any time
  • Google Permissions — Revoke STRDE's Google access at any time via Google Account Settings

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA — including the right to know what information is collected, the right to opt out of sale (we do not sell data), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address in Section 12. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

10

Children's Privacy

STRDE is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take prompt steps to delete that information.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you through an in-app notification or a prominent notice within the App
  • Where required by law, seek your renewed consent

Your continued use of STRDE after any changes to this Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
