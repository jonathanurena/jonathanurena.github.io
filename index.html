The Problem

Debt doesn't start with recklessness.
It starts with life.

A medical bill. A layoff. A baby that arrived before the savings did. Or simply the slow, quiet drift that happens when no one ever taught you how to manage money — because most of us weren't.

Millions of people carry debt not because they're irresponsible, but because the system around them was never designed to help them win. The apps that exist either overwhelm them with automation they don't trust, or collect their spending data and sell it to the same advertisers nudging them to spend more.

The people who need help the most are being quietly failed — and sometimes exploited — by the very tools promising to help them.

"What if someone finally built a tool that was completely, unconditionally on the user's side?"

The Idea

We built Fiinly for the person who's tired of being afraid to look at their bank account.

Fiinly is a budgeting and debt-elimination app built around one radical idea: your financial data belongs to you. We don't touch it. We don't sell it. We don't profit from keeping you in the cycle. We just help you get out.

It's simple enough to use in 10 seconds. It's honest enough that people actually trust it. And it's the first app designed not just to show you where your money went — but to show you the exact date you'll finally be free of debt, if you follow your plan.

That date changes everything. Debt stops feeling like a fog and starts feeling like a finish line.

Our Promise

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Most financial apps live inside data ecosystems where your spending habits have value — to someone else. That's not a conspiracy. It's a business model. And it's one that quietly works against the very person the app claims to serve.

Fiinly's model is different. We make money when you pay for the app. That's it. Your data never leaves your device. We don't know what you spend. We don't want to. Because the moment we start profiting from your habits, we stop being your ally.

We want to be the most trusted name in personal finance — not the most profitable one.

Built for Real Life

Money is rarely just one person's problem.

That's why Fiinly integrates with Apple Family Sharing. Couples, households, and families can operate on a single plan — sharing budgets, assigning responsibilities, and working toward the same goals without the friction that comes from financial miscommunication.

Because when a family gets aligned on their finances, something quietly shifts. Stress lowers. Trust deepens. And for the first time, they feel like they're on the same team.

Why This Matters

We're not asking you to believe in an app. We're asking you to believe in people.

Financial stress is one of the leading causes of anxiety, broken relationships, and lost opportunity in this country. It touches every part of a person's life — their health, their sleep, their sense of self-worth.

Fiinly won't solve all of that. But it can give someone something they may have never had: a clear, honest picture of where they stand — and a realistic, private, trustworthy path forward.

That's what we're building. Not just an app. A moment of relief for someone who's been carrying this weight alone for too long.

We believe there's a version of financial technology that's genuinely good for people. We're building it. And we'd love for you to help shape it with us.
